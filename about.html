The D@uzler Platform

With data analysis, you will be able to discover the value in your data within your company or organization. In order to have a productive, cost-effective analysis strategy that delivers actionable results, you will need hardware that is optimized for use with your software.

Today, data analysis encompasses a wide range of information technology - from special analysis platforms to databases to deep learning and artificial intelligence (AI).

An organized data pipeline facilitates business intelligence and enables you to analyze data effectively on a large scale and identify trends.

The latest technologies enable previously unused data sources, including unstructured data as well as data from intelligent edge systems to be included in your analysis environment.

When raw data is transformed into processed formats, a data pipeline is created. Intel technology makes it possible for companies or organizations to profit from their data at every stage of the pipeline.
